How It Works

Three steps to wine
confidence.

No more guessing. No more googling bottles one by one. Pocket Somm does the work of a trained sommelier in seconds.

01 📷
Scan the wine list

Open the app and point your camera at any restaurant wine list or wine bottle label. Tap to capture — the whole menu at once.

02 ✦
AI identifies every wine

Our GPT-4o powered engine reads and identifies every wine on the menu instantly — vintages, producers, regions and all.

03 🍷
Know everything instantly

See Vivino community scores, Wine Spectator critic ratings, tasting notes, food pairings, and retail prices — all in one screen.
